FAQ

Stuff People Ask Us

Real answers. No runaround.

Q.
How long does a roof replacement take?

Most homes in the Bay Area take 1 to 3 days depending on size, pitch, and weather. We give you an honest timeline before we start — and we stick to it.

Q.
Do you handle permits?

Yes. For any work requiring a permit in CA, we handle the entire process from filing to final inspection. You don't have to deal with the city.

Q.
Are you licensed and insured in California?

Fully licensed in CA, bonded, and insured. We can send you a certificate of insurance before work starts if needed — just ask.

Q.
Do you offer financing?

Yes. We have flexible financing options for larger projects. Ask us when you get your estimate — we want to make it work for your budget.

Q.
How much does a new roof cost in the Bay Area?

Most residential roofs in the Bay Area run $10,000 to $25,000 depending on size, slope, and materials. We give you exact numbers after seeing the job — no guessing.

Q.
Do you work on ADUs?

Yes. ADUs are one of our specialties. We handle the entire process — permits, foundation, framing, roofing, interior finish. One crew, one point of contact.
